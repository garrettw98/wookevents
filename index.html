Frequently Asked Questions

What is an Early Bird ticket?

An Early Bird ticket is a special ticket offered at a discounted price to a limited number of attendees. These tickets not only secure your spot at the festival but also make you part of our unique profit-sharing model.

How does the profit-sharing work for Early Bird ticket holders?

After the event, we calculate the net profit. Early Bird ticket holders are eligible to receive up to 50% of the total profit, divided equally among all Early Bird tickets. This amount is capped at the original ticket price, potentially allowing you to attend the festival for free.

What happens if the event doesn't make a profit?

In the unlikely event that we don't make a profit, Early Bird ticket holders will still have their festival experience. However, there would be no additional returns on the ticket price.

How and when will I receive my share of the profits?

Profits will be distributed electronically to the payment method you used for your Early Bird ticket purchase. This process typically occurs within 30-45 days after the event, allowing time for all financial reconciliations.

Can I transfer or sell my Early Bird ticket?

Early Bird tickets are non-transferable and non-refundable. This policy ensures that the profit-sharing benefit goes to our early supporters who took the initial risk with us.

How does buying an Early Bird ticket help the festival?

Early Bird ticket sales provide crucial upfront capital that helps us secure venues, book artists, and plan a better overall experience. Your early support allows us to make key decisions with confidence.

What makes Wook Events different from other festival organizers?

We believe in creating a more engaged and invested festival community. Our unique Early Bird model allows attendees to potentially benefit financially from the event's success, aligning the interests of organizers and festival-goers.

Are there any risks involved in purchasing an Early Bird ticket?

While we strive to make every event successful, there's always a risk in event planning. Early Bird ticket holders should be prepared for the possibility of no additional returns beyond the festival experience itself.

How can I get more information or ask additional questions?

If you have any questions that aren't answered here, please don't hesitate to reach out to us at contact@wookevents.com. We're always happy to provide more information and assist our community members.
